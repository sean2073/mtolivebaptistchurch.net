Our Pastor

Antonio Wilkerson is the head pastor of Mt. Olive Baptist Church in Plant City, Florida. Pastor Wilkerson married his lovely wife, Shirley Ann on November 3 of 1990 and are the proud parents of 2 adult children: Antonika and Antonio, Jr. with 5 grandchildren: Markayla, D’Quan, Talayjah, Arianna and Adian.

As a native of Tampa, Florida, he is a product of the Hillsborough County School System, although graduating from the Balboa High School in the Republic of Panama. When he received the call to ministry, he attended Blessed Hope Bible College where he received his doctorate in Christian Theology with a concentration in Christian Education. He also has graduated from Faith Bible Institute.

On November 13, 2016, Pastor Wilkerson was given the honor and task of being the shepherd of Mt. Olive Baptist Church of Plant City. Prior to uniting with Mt. Olive Baptist Church, Pastor Wilkerson served at Macedonia Missionary Baptist Church in Thonotosassa under Pastor W.L. Jackson at 1st Baptist Church of Progress Village in Tampa under the Dr. Sam Maxwell.
